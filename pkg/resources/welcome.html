Deli DL-888D PRO – macOS Driver

This installer sets up a native CUPS driver for the Deli DL-888D PRO thermal label printer.

What it does:

  • Installs the rastertotspl filter into /usr/libexec/cups/filter
  • Installs the PPD into /Library/Printers/PPDs/Contents/Resources
  • Reloads CUPS
  • Auto-detects the printer over USB and creates a queue called Deli_DL888D_PRO

After install, test from Terminal:

echo "Hallo" | lp -d Deli_DL888D_PRO
